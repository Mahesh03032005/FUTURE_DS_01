## ğŸ§© Dashboard Components

### 1. Sales by Sub-Category

* Bar chart showing **Sum of Sales** for each sub-category
* Helps identify top-performing and low-performing products

### 2. Discount by Category

* Column chart representing **Sum of Discount** across categories
* Useful to analyze which category receives higher discounts

### 3. Sales by Region

* Pie chart showing **regional contribution to total sales**
* Regions included: West, East, Central, South

### 4. Profit by Category

* Horizontal bar chart showing **Sum of Profit** by category
* Clearly highlights the most profitable category

### 5. Quantity by Category

* Donut chart representing **Sum of Quantity sold** per category
* Shows sales volume distribution

### 6. Sales by Category

* Pie chart visualizing **total sales share** of each category
